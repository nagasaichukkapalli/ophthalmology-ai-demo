Disease: Conjunctivitis
Symptoms: Redness, itching, gritty sensation, discharge, tearing
Description: Inflammation of the conjunctiva, often caused by infection or allergy. Also known as pink eye.
Specialist: General Ophthalmologist or Optometrist

Disease: Glaucoma
Symptoms: Gradual loss of peripheral vision, tunnel vision, eye pain, blurred vision, halos around lights
Description: A group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, often caused by abnormally high pressure in your eye.
Specialist: Glaucoma Specialist

Disease: Cataract
Symptoms: Clouded, blurred or dim vision, increasing difficulty with vision at night, sensitivity to light and glare, fading or yellowing of colors
Description: A clouding of the clear lens of the eye.
Specialist: Cataract Surgeon

Disease: Diabetic Retinopathy
Symptoms: Spots or dark strings floating in vision (floaters), blurred vision, fluctuating vision, dark or empty areas in vision, vision loss
Description: A diabetes complication that affects eyes. It's caused by damage to the blood vessels of the light-sensitive tissue at the back of the eye (retina).
Specialist: Retina Specialist

Disease: Uveitis
Symptoms: Eye redness, eye pain, light sensitivity, blurred vision, dark, floating spots in field of vision (floaters), decreased vision
Description: A form of eye inflammation. It affects the middle layer of tissue in the eye wall (uvea).
Specialist: Uveitis Specialist or Retina Specialist
